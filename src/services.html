Pool Area

You don't have to be a pro to use the swimming pool at GrecFitnessClub, as all of our gyms with pools offer expert swim tuition.

Classes run for 30–60 minutes and are available for all abilities – from total beginners to those just wanting to improve their technique. Non-members welcome! Contact your local club for further details.

The swimming pools are complimented by relaxing hot spa pools to soothe away the aches and stresses of the day, along with truly luxurious beautiful fibre optic lit steam rooms to complete the relaxation program - you won't want to go home!
